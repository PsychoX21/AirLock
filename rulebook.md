# Airlock: District Manager Manual

**Welcome to Airlock!** 
You take on the role of a District Manager trying to protect your neighborhoods from the relentless pollution spewing out of the city’s central factory.

## Pollutants
1. **PM10 - Coarse Particles (Yellow Circles):** "PM" stands for Particulate Matter, and "10" means 10 micrometers. These are relatively large particles like **Dust, Pollen, Spores, or Ash**. They get trapped in your throat and nose.
2. **PM2.5 - Fine Particles (Red Triangles):** Smaller than 2.5 micrometers—about 1/30th the width of a human hair! These are dangerous particles like **Smoke, Soot, SO4 (Sulfate), and NO3 (Nitrate)**. They are so tiny they can enter your bloodstream directly through your lungs.
3. **GAS - Toxic Fumes (Purple Octagons):** Invisible but deadly gases like **Carbon Monoxide (CO), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), or Sulfur Dioxide (SO2)** produced by burning chemical fuels.

---

## Game Objective
The game lasts for 10 rounds (or 15 for a longer game). Most **Victory Points (VP)** wins! 
- **Earn +1 VP** every time you successfully capture (purify) a pollutant from the air.
- **+5 VP Bonus:** Spotless District (0 tokens at end).
- **+3 VP Bonus:** Mostly Clean District (1-2 tokens at end).

**Components:**
- **Board:** Central Factory, 6 lanes (Core, 3 Filter Zones, District).
- **Tokens:** PM10 (Yellow Circle), PM2.5 (Red Triangle), Gas (Purple Octagon).
- **Filter Cards:** Catch specific shapes. Shows Cost / Sell Value.
- **Climate Cards:** Random round modifiers.
- **Funding (SC):** Currency for buying filters.

**Setup:**
1. **Lanes:** 2-Player = 3 alternating lanes each. 3-Player = 2 adjacent lanes each.
2. **Funding:** 12 SC per player (10 SC in 3-Player).
3. **Market:** Deal 5 Filter Cards face-up.
4. **Round Tracker:** Pawn on Round 1.

**Turn Structure (3 Phases):**

**Phase 1: Emission**
1. **Climate Event:** (Rounds 4, 9, 14 only) Draw 1 Climate Card. Read effect for the round.
2. **Windrose:** Spin center arrow. Determines starting lane.
3. **Draw Emission:** Draw 1 card from current Tier. Spawns tokens in Factory Core.
   - *Tier 1 (Green):* Rounds 1-5. 
   - *Tier 2 (Yellow):* Rounds 6-10. 
   - *Tier 3 (Red):* Rounds 11-15 (Extended game only).
4. **Push Pollution:** Push new tokens into lanes starting at windrose, advancing clockwise 1 lane per token.
   - **CASCADING LIMITS:** Core = Max 2 tokens. Zones 1, 2, 3 = Max 1 token each. District = Infinite.
   - *Cascade Rule:* If pushing into a full slot, MUST physically slide the oldest token outwards into the next slot!

**Phase 2: Action**
- Players take turns clockwise. **2 Action Points (AP)** per turn.
- **Draft (1 AP + SC Cost):** Buy 1 Filter from market (pay SC). Replenish market immediately.
- **Place/Replace & Sell (0 AP - FREE!):** Place Filter from hand into any of your Filter Zones.
   - *Overwrite:* Replacing an old filter? Discard old card, instantly gain its **Sell Value** in SC!
   - *Tier 3 Upgrade:* In Rounds 11-15, each Zone can hold **2 Filter Cards** instead of 1!
   - *Instant Catch:* Placing under a compatible token? Catch it immediately! (+1 SC, Keep token for +1 VP).
- **Purify (1 AP):** Roll Purify Die. Number = Max tokens you can clear this turn.
   - Take token from compatible Filter Card in your lane.
   - **Keep token (worth 1 VP)!** Gain +1 SC per token.
- **Deflect (1 AP, MAX 1/turn):** Push 1 token from any of your Filter Zones (not Core!) to Zone 1 of opponent's lane that has the *least* active pollution. (Triggers Cascading Rule if full).

**Phase 3: Upkeep**
1. **Income:** All players gain 3 SC (2 SC in 3-Player).
2. **Advance:** Move Round Tracker up 1 space.
